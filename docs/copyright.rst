About Vistle
============

Vistle development is lead by the `visualization department <https://www.hlrs.de/about/organization/management-staff/visualization>`__  at `HLRS <https://www.hlrs.de>`__ since 2012 and can be followed in its `source code repository <https://github.com/vistle/vistle/>`__.

Funding has been provided, among others, by the projects `CRESTA <http://www.cresta-project.eu>`__, `bwVisu <https://www.hlrs.de/projects/detail/bwvisu>`__, `EXCELLERAT <https://www.hlrs.de/projects/detail/excellerat>`__, `KoLabBW <https://www.kolabbw.hlrs.de/>`__ and `CEEC <https://ceec-coe.eu>`__.
